CFD tests

Taking the flag as a rigid object, we perform 3 subtests focusing on the fluid dynamics part of the problem. The flag can be made almost rigid by setting the structural parameters to large values (, ) or completely rigid by considering the flow domain only with fixed boundary conditions on the flag interface.
